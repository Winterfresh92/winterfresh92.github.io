Welcome to "Journey for The Eye", an interactive tale by Kevin Stubblefield! I hope you enjoy the adventure!
Use the command line beneath the text box to enter commands. You'll know commands because they'll be in bold. Depending on your screen size, you may not be able to see a full amount of text after your command. If that's the case, scroll up until you see a divider between your response and the story text. The game will begin with a few prompts to create your character.
